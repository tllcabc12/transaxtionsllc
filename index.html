Flagship Project · 2025–2026

The Kothi Raj
El Capitan Project

transaxtions llc is the U.S. petitioning and production entity for the Kothi Raj El Capitan Project — bringing Jyothi Raj E. ("Kothi Raj"), the Spiderman of Karnataka, to attempt The Nose route on El Capitan in Yosemite National Park.

A self-taught free solo climber who rose from extraordinary hardship in Chitradurga, Kothi Raj carries a tradition of superhuman climbing rooted in the Anjaneya heritage of Karnataka. His story is being developed into a full documentary, with a U.S. cultural tour visiting Indian diaspora communities nationwide.

transaxtions llc has formally extended an invitation to Kothi Raj to visit the United States for this historic attempt and cultural tour — the first step in bringing this extraordinary story to American audiences.
